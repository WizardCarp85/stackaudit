### FAQ

**1. How much does StackAudit cost?**
StackAudit is 100% free to use. Our goal is to help startups optimize their runway. We make money only if you choose to purchase discounted AI credits through our parent marketplace, Credex.

**2. Do I need to connect my credit card or AWS account?**
No. StackAudit is completely manual and secure. You simply select the tools your team uses and input your seat counts. We never ask for API keys, billing credentials, or financial integrations.

**3. How accurate are the cost savings?**
Extremely accurate. Our audit engine runs on deterministic math using the official, up-to-date pricing data from providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, and GitHub. 

**4. What happens to my audit data?**
Your audit data is stored locally on your device for a frictionless experience. We only collect your email if you explicitly choose to save your results or sign up for alerts on discounted credits.

**5. Which AI tools do you currently support?**
We currently audit the most popular enterprise AI tools, including Cursor, GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT Plus/Team, Claude Pro/Team, and API usage estimates for Anthropic and OpenAI.
